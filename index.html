Technical Events

  • PAPER EXPO

    The paper presentation consists of 2 rounds. The abstract is collected from the participants in online mode and the shortlisted abstracts will be allowed to present with their PPT in front of jury panel.

  • DESIGNOPS EXPO

    Beauty lies in the eyes of the beholder and a wonderful interface experience lies in the hands of a user. Get ready to showcase your designing skills and compete against some of the sharpest UI/UX designers!

  • INNOSPECTRA

    The project competition consists of 2 rounds. The abstract is collected from the participants in online mode and the shortlisted projects will be allowed to present with their prototype in front of jury panel.

  • INNO-QUEST

    Are you ready to ignite your creative spark and be part of an innovation revolution? Join us for Inno-Quest Ideathon, a high-energy event that celebrates innovation, fosters creativity, and transforms ideas into actionable solutions.

Non-Technical Events

  • MYSTERY UNEARTHED

    Are you a true crime enthusiast or a booming detective in your heart? Test your mysteries solving skills in our fascinating Mystery Crime Quiz Challenge.

  • NETWORK NEXUS

    The Ultimate Connection Game Challenge" is a thrilling event that immerses participants in the world of connectivity. This gathering is designed to test their skills in both physical and digital connections, fostering competition

  • NARRATIVE CARNIVAL

    The Story Building Competition offers an exhilarating and innovative storytelling challenge. Participants will face unique prompts in each round, testing their creativity and adaptability.

  • RECALL RUMBLE

    Recall Rumble is a thrilling memory challenge where participants dive headfirst into a world of brain-teasing puzzles, rapid- fire recall tasks, and memory-enhancing exercises.
